<strong style="font-size: 1.1em;">Honor: First Prize at the 19th National Robot Competition for College Students</strong>

*In the RMUC, participating teams will independently develop different types of robots with various functions to face off on the battlefield. Each team’s participants will control their robots to attack the opponent’s robots and Base by launching projectiles. At the end of the match, the team with the highest remaining Base HP wins.*

Join [HITCRT](https://sme.hit.edu.cn/2023/1214/c18324a336453/page.htm) team of Harbin Institute of Technology and researched precise and rapid activation of the Power Rune to support the whole team in the match, achieving successful activation within 5 seconds finally. 

The research primarily involved <strong style="font-size: 1.0em;">image target detection </strong>and <strong style="font-size: 1.0em;">target motion estimation </strong>.
