Honor: First Prize at the 19th National College Student Robot Competition

In the RMUC, participating teams will independently develop different types of robots with various functions to face off on the battlefield. Each team’s participants will control their robots to attack the opponent’s robots and Base by launching projectiles. At the end of the match, the team with the highest remaining Base HP wins.

Researched precise and rapid activation of the Power Rune to support the whole team in the [HITCRT](https://baike.baidu.com/item/%E5%93%88%E5%B0%94%E6%BB%A8%E5%B7%A5%E4%B8%9A%E5%A4%A7%E5%AD%A6%E7%AB%9E%E6%8A%80%E6%9C%BA%E5%99%A8%E4%BA%BA%E9%98%9F/15565648) team of Harbin Institute of Technology, achieving successful activation within 5 seconds finally. The research primarily involved image target detection and target motion estimation.
